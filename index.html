Recent Trips

Highest Point in the Rockies

At 14,439 feet, Mt. Elbert should be a relatively easy climb in good conditions. Our October summit bid faced 50mph wind and sub-zero temps above the treeline, making for a chilly challenge. Sipped cocoa in Vail under 8" of snow on our recovery day.

Decalibron

Initial goal was to touch the summits of Mt. Democrat, Cameron, Lincoln, and Bross in one day with a 3am start. Deep snow up and down Democrat (in June!) zapped us and we turned down after Cameron. We'll never forget epic human sledding on the descent.

Island Recalibration

Snorkeling, mini-boat racing, and a bioluminescent algae swim augmented afternoons getting our minds right on the beaches of Fajardo, Puerto Rico. Love a weekend trip to our favorite American territory.

On a Quandary

Set up basecamp near a hot tub in Breckenridge with the goal of shooting up Quandary Peak (14,265'). Views at the summit doctored our souls, while the descent in deep, late-season slush certainly built our character.
